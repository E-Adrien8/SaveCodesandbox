About the Kingdom

Aeetes is the king of the region of Colchis (present-day Georgia), 1375 kilometers from Iolcos. He is the guardian of the Golden Fleece and will challenge Jason to retrieve the Fleece. As a reward he promises her the hand of his daughter with the blessing of Queen Idyia.
